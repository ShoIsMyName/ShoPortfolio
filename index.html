About me


Hello, my name is Sho. I am a student at Roi-Et Wittayalai School, majoring in Science, Mathematics, and Computers. I am very interested in computers, especially in complicated or difficult-to-understand subjects. I like them very much.

Currently, I have chosen what I like the most, which is cybersecurity, because it can do something useful for everyone who uses the internet, including my loved ones, because I want them to be safe from people with malicious intentions on the internet (hackers).

What inspires me the most is my family, because they have always supported me. No matter how big the obstacles in my work are, they are always there to encourage me. So I have a mindset of ​​"who I'm doing this for?". I would say ​"for my family".

The reason I want to work in cybersecurity is because I want everyone who uses the Internet or networks to be more secure. My family and I were once victims of hackers and call centers who tricked us out of money. But when we reported it to the police, the case did not move forward. So I thought that there were no traces that could be tracked (poor security system) or the agency was not qualified. So I realized that if one day we encounter something like this again, will we have to keep struggling like this? That is why I am determined to seriously pursue a career in cybersecurity.
